Government Polytechnic Amravati

Year of Established  :  1955

Location  :  Amravati , Maharashtra

Principle  :  Dr. V.R.Mankar

About College  :  Government Polytechnic, Amravati is an autonomous Institute of Govt. Polytechnic, Maharashtra established in the year 1955. This institute has a long history of producing technical manpower and rendering technical services to the society. Many students of this institute are chairing the topmost positions in various govt. Few of the students became successful entrepreneur looking to the overall performance of the institute. MSBTE, Mumbai has awarded academic autonomy to the institute from 1995.
